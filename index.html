Caption

the lone bear scuttles
until a sudden portal
a glorious fall

How is the artwork created?

I decided to use the sculpting tool within Blender to create a sitting bear! It took much patience and persistence, but using primarily the smoothing, inflating, scraping, flattening, layering, and elastic deform tool, I was able to turn a sphere into a bear! I did run into an issue of non-manifold vertices, but I was able to fix that through Blender's "Select All By Trait" tool. There were additional issues with rendering the .dae file within the Mesh Editor, but with the help of a fellow student on Piazza, I was able to change the Lambert shading to Phong shading and switch the triangles geometry to polylist with a vcount tag. I then took various screenshots of the subdivided bear in the MeshEditor, incrementally moving the bear. 20 screenshots later, I imported them into Photoshop and attempted to use some of the twelve basic principles of animation that we learned about in lecture and edited the background of the bear for each frame. Finally, I imported the frames into an online GIF maker and produced the final GIF!

How long did it take?

Around 10 hours
